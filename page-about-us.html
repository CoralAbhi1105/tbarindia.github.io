About Us

About us

T-Bar offers tailor made franchise models for investors. We are staffed by a team of experienced Market Researchers, Analysts, Brand Consultants and headed by leaders with extensive industry experience of 2 decades in various business segments, with core competency in establishing New Start-ups, Brand Consulting, Franchise distribution and expansion.

Franchise Concept

T-Bar opens up a unique platform for aspiring YOUNG ENTERPRENEURS to invest and make their future in F&B industry. As a brand T-Bar is open for a pan India presence, opening doors for all young people across the country to start their career and be a part of the fast-growing F&B industry. Tea has a special place in life of Indian people and as Indians we dream of opening our own café or outlets. T-Bar India brings the opportunity to fulfill your dream at a competitive cost. It has various options to fit in your budget and requirement. It is an extremely promising business with a high return at a lesser investment in comparison to any other F&B business segment. We provide you with 24 hours franchise support and understand your needs and requirements completely.

T-Bar encourages YOUNG WOMEN ENTREPRENEURS to start their own café and become financially independent. T-Bar promotes Women Empowerment and hence gives benefits to women entrepreneurs to use their talent and skill in making the maximum earning out of the business. We encourage women to start their own business or café at a lesser investment as compared to other tea franchise brands.

Our vision and mission

T-Bar wishes to have a pan India presence and also make its Franchise presence noticeable in the global F&B industry. It aims to expand its franchise across the globe. T-Bar besides expanding its own business wishes to provide support to every aspirant individual to become entrepreneurs. It aims to bring out the entrepreneur in you by providing the necessary support at a competitive budget which is affordable to most of the individuals who wish to break the shackles of a 9 to 5 job and start up something on their own.

T-Bar customised franchise format gives you the pedestal to bring forth your business skills. It wishes to spread out in every nook and corner of the country with its magical formula of tea - the most desired hot beverage at the current moment in the country.
