Master Franchise

Tea is India's Liquid Gold! It is available at every nook and corner of the country, on every street, state and region. That's why, when you choose to become the Master Franchisee of T-Bar, you are opening yourself to a pan-India market, free from geographical, or demographical boundaries. If you want all the perks, benefits and exclusive rights to T-Bar Cafe's growing popularity then you need to become our Master Franchisee.

If you are an experienced business person or have significant understanding of the food industry, then becoming a Master Franchisee will grant you master rights of the brand in one particular geographical region. A Master franchisee will also get a certain percentage of Franchise Fee, and royalty percentage from Unit Franchisee. Any inquiries of that geographical region received by T-Bar India will also be sent to the Master Franchisee.

Unit Franchise

We're growing fast, by gaining the trust of the tealovers and the communities we work with. We are currently looking to offer unit franchises in all parts of India.

With a competitive investment, you can become a Unit Franchise of T-Bar wherein we offer a complete start up kit which includes assistance in selection of location, conceptualization and finalizing of Design including General layout, 3D drawing and other technical drawings, Complete Fabrication, including interior designing, equipment set-up, staff recruitment & training, pre marketing and outlet launch. You will also get 15 days on-site support from our experienced team members, to help you get a smooth start.

All you will need is minimum 150 sq. ft. area at a busy location, like Hospitals, Universities, Corporate Houses, Commercial Buildings or busy markets. Our team will work with you to ensure that you have everything that you need to become a successful working unit of T-Bar Cafe's growing family!
